Call is now being recorded

Who even talks on the phone anymore?

Nessa and I talk on the phone about once a week. For a while, I'd wanted to record them because the conversations mean a lot to us. So, one day we just did. Our conversations explore the intricacies of daily life, and we tend to get out of our comfort zones. We invite you to get to know us in an organic way, because we're also getting to know ourselves in the process. They are as much for us as they are for you.
